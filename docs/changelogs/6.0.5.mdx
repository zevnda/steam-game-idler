### New 🎉
- **Sign in with Steam** now detects when the same account is signed in somewhere else (another device, or your real Steam client) - automation is paused right away and you're prompted to either reconnect or switch to a different account or sign-in method, instead of silently fighting the other session in the background
- Added support for the **Chinese Simplified** language. If you'd like to help translate SGI into your language, see the [contribution guide](https://github.com/zevnda/steam-game-idler/discussions/148)

### Improved 🚀
- Steam credential validation errors are now shown consistently inline within the credentials panel across **Card Farming**, **Inventory Manager**, and **Settings > Steam Credentials**, instead of appearing differently (or not at all) depending on where you entered them
- Added a **Clear** button to the Steam credentials panel in **Card Farming** and **Inventory Manager**, matching **Settings > Steam Credentials**

### Fixed 🐛
- Fixed a misleading "Your Steam Community session has expired" message shown in **Card Farming** and **Inventory Manager** when entering credentials that had never been valid in the first place, rather than a session that had actually expired
- Fixed an issue in **Settings > Steam Credentials** where a failed save could show no error message, or a leftover one from a previous attempt, instead of the actual error
- Fixed an issue in **Inventory Manager** where accounts using **Legacy Sign In** with no saved Steam credentials could keep showing a previously cached inventory list indefinitely instead of prompting to reconnect
- Fixed **Free Games** notifications and the sidebar's claimable indicator sometimes triggering for a game you already own, caused by the free games list loading before your owned games list had finished loading
- Fixed **Auto-redeem free games** silently never retrying a game that had already been seen once (e.g. before auto-redeem was turned on), even if it was never actually claimed
- Fixed a claimed free game sticking around in the **Free Games** tab instead of disappearing once it was claimed
- Fixed **Sign in with Steam** accounts occasionally being sent back to the sign-in screen on launch instead of resuming, even though the saved session was actually still valid
- Fixed a rare timing issue where a **Sign in with Steam** account could fail to reconnect and be silently swapped for a different signed-in account
