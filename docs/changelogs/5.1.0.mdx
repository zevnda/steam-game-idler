### New 🎉
- **Automatic Steam Account Switching**: Selecting a different account on the **Account Selection** screen now automatically switches accounts in the Steam client to match
  - This removes the friction of manually switching accounts in Steam when switching accounts in SGI — the two are now always kept in sync for you
- **Auto-Launch Steam**: If Steam isn't running when you click **Continue** on the sign-in screen, SGI will now launch it for you automatically
  - Previously, SGI would block you from continuing until Steam was manually opened — users no longer need to think about launch order

### Improved 🚀
- The **Steam is not running** warning modal now includes a **Continue** button that launches Steam directly and now also automatically dismisses itself once Steam is detected as running
