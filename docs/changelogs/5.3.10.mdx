### New 🎉
- **New PRO Gamer Tier Feature**: Added a **Run Multiple Games** setting to **Settings > Achievement Unlocker**
  - When enabled, SGI unlocks achievements for up to 32 games at the same time instead of one at a time, each with its own independent unlock schedule
- The **Achievement Unlocker** now shows a queue of the next 5 upcoming achievements, each with its own live countdown until unlock

### Improved 🚀
- Moved the **Currency** setting from **Settings > General** to **Settings > Inventory Manager** as this setting is only used by that feature

### Fixed 🐛
- Fixed an issue in **Inventory Manager** where Steam credentials were not being automatically revalidated for **PRO** users
