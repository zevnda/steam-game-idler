### Improved 🚀
- **Achievement Unlocker** now scans games much faster by reusing previously fetched achievement data instead of re-scanning every game each time

### Fixed 🐛
- Fixed **Achievement Unlocker** incorrectly logging achievements as unlocked (and removing games from the list) even when the unlock actually failed, when running many games concurrently
- Fixed a backend bottleneck in **Achievement Unlocker** that caused long delays and frequent failures when unlocking achievements for many games at once
