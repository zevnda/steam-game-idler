### New 🎉
- **PayPal Payment Method Added:** After many requests, **PayPal** is now available as a payment method for **PRO** subscriptions. When subscribing, you can now choose to checkout with either **Stripe** or **PayPal**
  - If you have an active Stripe subscription and would like to switch to PayPal, you can do so by canceling your current subscription and subscribing again using PayPal, or by contacting support via the in-app **Help Desk** or [contact@steamgameidler.com](mailto:contact@steamgameidler.com)
