### New 🎉
- **Auto Card Farming (PRO)**: A new **Auto Card Farming** setting has been added to **Settings → Card Farming**. When enabled, SGI automatically checks your library every 15 minutes for games with card drops remaining, adds them to your **Card Farming** list, and starts farming them

### Improved 🚀
- Improved the look and layout of some settings pages 

### Fixed 🐛
- Fixed an issue where SGI would try to revalidate the user's Steam credentials when the user had manually entered valid credentials already
