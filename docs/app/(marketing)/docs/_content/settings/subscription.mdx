# Subscription Settings

This tab manages your [Steam Game Idler PRO](/docs/pro) subscription. It doesn't offer a purchase flow itself — for how to subscribe, see [How to Subscribe to PRO](/docs/pro#faq).

## Status

Shows whether you're currently subscribed and which tier (**Casual** or **Gamer**), along with your renewal or cancellation date. Click **Manage** to open your billing portal (Stripe or PayPal, depending on how you subscribed) to update payment details, view invoices, or cancel.

## License Key

Once you've subscribed, you'll receive a license key by email.

- **Activate** — paste your license key and click **Activate** to unlock PRO on this device
- **Copy** — copies your currently-activated key to the clipboard
- **Clear** — removes the key from this device, deactivating PRO here (your subscription itself isn't cancelled — you can re-activate on any device later)

<Callout type="info">
    Your license key is tied to one device at a time. Activating it on a new device while it's already active elsewhere will prompt you to confirm transferring it — see [Transferring to a New Device](/docs/pro#faq)
</Callout>
