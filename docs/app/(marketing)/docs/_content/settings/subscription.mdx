# Subscription Settings

You'll find this tab under <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Subscription' />

## Status

Shows whether you're currently subscribed and which tier (**Casual** or **Gamer**), along with your renewal or cancellation date.

- Click <MockButton type='manage-subscription' /> to open your billing portal (Stripe or PayPal, depending on how you subscribed) to update payment details, view invoices, or cancel
- If you're on **Casual**, an <MockButton type='upgrade' /> button also appears next to it to move up to **Gamer**

## License Key

Once you've subscribed, you'll receive a license key by email.

- **Activate**: paste your license key and click <MockButton type='activate' /> to unlock PRO on this device
- **Copy**: click <MockButton type='copy' /> to copy your currently-activated key to the clipboard
- **Clear**: click <MockButton type='clear' /> to remove the key from this device, deactivating PRO here (your subscription itself isn't cancelled, you can re-activate on any device later)

<Callout type="info">
    Your license key is tied to one device at a time. Activating it on a new device while it's already active elsewhere will prompt you to confirm transferring it. See [Transferring to a New Device](/docs/pro#faq)
</Callout>
