# Free Games Settings

## Free Game Notifications
Get a Windows notification when a new free Steam game becomes available

SGI periodically checks for new free games and will notify you when one appears. Note that notifications may not be immediate. There can be a short delay depending on when SGI last checked.

To avoid being notified about games you already own, make sure your games list is up to date by clicking <MockButton type='content' content='Refresh' /> in <MockButton type='your-games' />.

## Auto Redeem Free Games
Automatically add free Steam games to your account as soon as they become available *(Pro Gamer tier)*

When enabled, SGI will claim free games on your behalf without any input from you. What else this requires depends on how you [signed in](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in):

- **Signed in with Steam**: no extra step needed, just turn this on
- **Legacy Sign In**: you must also click <MockButton type='content' content='Sign in to Steam' /> on this tab first, and stay signed in, for auto-redeem to work

<Callout type="info">
  If you enable Auto Redeem, consider also enabling [Free Game Notifications](#free-game-notifications) so you can see which games were claimed on your behalf.
</Callout>
