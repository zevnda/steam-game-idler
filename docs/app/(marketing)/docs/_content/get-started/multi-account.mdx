# Multi-Account

SGI can keep several **Steam Sign-in** accounts signed in and running at the same time, each with its own idling, card farming, and achievement unlocking happening independently. You switch between them from one app window without signing out.

## How Many Accounts Can I Add?

The number of **Steam Sign-in** accounts you can have signed in at the same time depends on your [subscription tier](/docs/pro):

| | Free | Casual | Gamer
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Concurrent Steam Sign-in accounts | 1 | 3 | 10 |

<Callout type='info'>
  You can still have one **Legacy Sign-in** account signed in alongside your **Steam Sign-in** accounts, regardless of tier. It doesn't count against this limit
</Callout>

## Adding an Account

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open the account switcher
    Click your account name/avatar at the bottom of the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Add another account
    Click <MockButton type='add-account' />
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Sign in
    Follow the same [Steam Sign-in](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in#steam-sign-in) or [Legacy Sign-in](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in#legacy-sign-in) steps for the new account
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching Between Accounts

Click your account name/avatar at the bottom of the sidebar, then click the account you want to switch to. Every account you've added keeps idling, farming, and unlocking in the background while you're viewing a different one. Switching only changes which account's dashboard you're looking at.

## Signing Out

Click your account name/avatar at the bottom of the sidebar and click <MockButton type='sign-out' /> on the account you no longer want signed in.
