# Multi-Account

SGI can keep several **Sign in with Steam** accounts signed in and running at the same time — each with its own idling, card farming, and achievement unlocking happening independently. You switch between them from one app window without signing out.

<Callout type='info'>
    This only applies to accounts signed in with [Sign in with Steam](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in#sign-in-with-steam). A [Legacy Sign In](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in#legacy-sign-in) account is always limited to one at a time — that's a limitation of the Steam client itself, not SGI
</Callout>

## How Many Accounts Can I Add?

The number of **Sign in with Steam** accounts you can have signed in *at the same time* depends on your [subscription tier](/docs/pro):

| Tier | Concurrent Sign in with Steam accounts |
|---|:---:|
| Free | 1 |
| Casual | 3 |
| Gamer | 10 |

This limit only applies to how many accounts can be **signed in and active simultaneously** — you can freely sign out of one account and into a different one any number of times on the Free tier.

<Callout type='info'>
  You can still have one **Legacy Sign In** account signed in alongside your **Sign in with Steam** accounts, regardless of tier — it doesn't count against this limit
</Callout>

## Adding an Account

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open the account switcher
    Click your account name/avatar at the bottom of the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Add another account
    Click **Add another account**
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Sign in
    Follow the same [Sign in with Steam](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in#sign-in-with-steam) or [Legacy Sign In](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in#legacy-sign-in) steps for the new account
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching Between Accounts

Click your account name/avatar at the bottom of the sidebar, then click the account you want to switch to. Every account you've added keeps idling, farming, and unlocking in the background while you're viewing a different one — switching only changes which account's dashboard you're looking at.

## Signing Out

Open the account switcher and choose to sign out the account you no longer want signed in. Signing out an account stops all of its running automations (idling, card farming, achievement unlocking) — the other signed-in accounts are unaffected.
