# How to Claim Free Steam Games Automatically

The Free Games feature allows you to discover and claim free games on Steam, either manually or automatically. This is perfect for users who want to expand their game library without spending money.

<DocsCTA />

<Callout type="info">
  DLC, free-to-play games, and games that you already own are not shown in the Free Games list
</Callout>

## Manual Redemption

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='free-games' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='steam' /> beneath the game image to open its Steam store page where you can add the game to your library
  </Step>

  <Step>
    After claiming a free game, refresh your games list clicking <MockButton type='content' content='Refresh' /> in the page header of <MockButton type='your-games' />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automated Redemption

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='content' content='Sign In via Steam' /> in <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Free Games' /> and sign in to your Steam account in the new window via:
    - Username and password
    - Scanning the QR Code
  </Step>

  <Step>
    As free games become available, SGI will automatically attempt to redeem them to your Steam account
  </Step>
</Steps>
