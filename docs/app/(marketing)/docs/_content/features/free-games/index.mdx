# How to Claim Free Steam Games Automatically

The Free Games feature allows you to discover and claim free games on Steam, either manually or automatically. This is perfect for users who want to expand their game library without spending money.

<Callout type="info">
  DLC, free-to-play games, and games that you already own are not shown in the Free Games list
</Callout>

## Manual Redemption

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='free-games' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click the <MockButton type='content' content='Claim' /> button on individual games to claim them
  </Step>

  <Step>
    A Steam store login window will open, sign in to your Steam account in the new window via:
    - Username and password
    - Scanning the QR Code

    Optionally, if you would prefer to not sign into your Steam account, you can visit the game's Steam store page in your browser and claim it manually.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    After claiming a free game, refresh your games list by clicking <MockButton type='content' content='Refresh' /> in the page header of <MockButton type='your-games' />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automated Redemption

How you enable this depends on how you [signed in](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in)

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Steam Sign-in
    Just enable [Auto Redeem Free Games](/docs/settings/free-games#auto-redeem-free-games) in <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Free Games' />, no separate sign-in step needed
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Legacy Sign-in
    Go to <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Free Games' /> and click <MockButton type='content' content='Sign in to Steam' /> and sign in to your Steam account in the new window via:
    - Username and password
    - Scanning the QR Code
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Automatic Claiming
    As free games become available, SGI will automatically attempt to redeem them to your Steam account
  </Step>
</Steps>
