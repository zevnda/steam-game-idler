# Favorites

Favorites lets you pin specific games to their own page for quick access, separate from your full library.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='favorites' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Go to the <MockButton type='all-games' /> tab and click on individual games to add or remove them as favorites
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Switch to the <MockButton type='content' content='List' /> tab to see your favorited games. Drag and drop them by grabbing the <MockButton type='grabber' /> to reorder the list
  </Step>
</Steps>

To remove every favorited game at once, click <MockButton type='content' content='Clear favorites' /> in the page header.

<Callout type='info'>
    Favoriting a game doesn't start idling it, farm cards for it, or unlock its achievements. It's purely a shortcut list. Use [Idling](/docs/features/idling), [Card Farming](/docs/features/card-farming), or the [Achievement Unlocker](/docs/features/achievement-unlocker) for that
</Callout>
