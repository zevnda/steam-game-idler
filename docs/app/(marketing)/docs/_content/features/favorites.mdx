# Favorites

Favorites lets you pin specific games to their own page for quick access, separate from your full library.

<Callout type='info'>
    Favoriting a game doesn't start idling it, farm cards for it, or unlock its achievements. It's purely a shortcut list. Use [Playtime Booster](/docs/features/playtime-booster), [Card Farming](/docs/features/card-farming), or the [Achievement Unlocker](/docs/features/achievement-unlocker) for that
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='favorites' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Go to the <MockButton type='all-games' /> tab and click the <MockButton type='context-add' /> button on individual games to add them to your favorites
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Switch to the <MockButton type='content' content='Favorites' /> tab to see your favorited games
  </Step>
</Steps>
