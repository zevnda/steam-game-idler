# Blacklisting Games

You can blacklist specific games from being farmed for cards by going to the <MockButton type='all-games' /> tab and clicking the ban button on individual games.

Blacklisted games will not be farmed for cards, even if they have card drops remaining and even if they're in your queue — a blacklisted game is also automatically removed from the queue.

To view or un-blacklist games, switch to the <MockButton type='content' content='Blacklisted' /> tab.
