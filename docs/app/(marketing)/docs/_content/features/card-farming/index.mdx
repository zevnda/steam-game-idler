# How to Farm Steam Trading Cards Automatically

The Card Farming feature allows you to automatically farm trading cards from your Steam games without needing to download or play them. This is ideal for users looking to collect cards for badge crafting or marketplace sales efficiently.

<DocsCTA />

<Callout type="info">
    You must add your [Steam Credentials](/docs/steam-credentials) before you can use this feature — unless you [signed in with Steam](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in) on a **Gamer** tier account, in which case this happens automatically
</Callout>

Card Farming has three tabs: **Games With Drops** (every eligible game, browse and queue from here), **Queue** (games you've chosen to farm), and **Blacklisted** (games excluded from farming entirely).

### Farm Specific Games

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='card-farming' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Go to the <MockButton type='all-games' /> tab and click on individual games to add them to your queue
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Switch to the <MockButton type='content' content='Queue' /> tab to review what you've added, then click <MockButton type='card-farming-action' />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
    Only games in your **Queue** tab are farmed when you start — adding a game to the queue doesn't start farming by itself. If you'd rather farm every eligible game in your library without queuing them individually, enable [Farm All Games](/docs/settings/card-farming#farm-all-games) instead
</Callout>

### Blacklist a Game

Blacklisting removes a game from farming entirely, even if it still has card drops remaining. See [Blacklisting Games](/docs/features/card-farming/blacklisting-games) for details.

### Automatic Farming *(Pro — Gamer tier)*

Enable [Auto Farm Cards](/docs/settings/card-farming#auto-farm-cards) in <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Card Farming' /> to have SGI automatically farm every queued game with drops remaining on a recurring schedule, with no need to start it manually.
