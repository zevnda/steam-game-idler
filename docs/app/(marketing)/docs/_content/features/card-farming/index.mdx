# How to Farm Steam Trading Cards Automatically

The Card Farming feature allows you to automatically farm trading cards from your Steam games without needing to download or play them. This is ideal for users looking to collect cards for badge crafting or marketplace sales efficiently.

<Callout type="info">
    You must add your [Steam Credentials](/docs/steam-credentials) before you can use this feature.
    
    Users using [Steam Sign-in](/docs/get-started/how-to-sign-in) with a [Gamer tier subscription](/pro) do not need to manually enter their Steam credentials.
</Callout>

### Farm All Games With Drops Remaining

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Card Farming' /> and enabled <MockButton type='content' content='Farm all games' />
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='card-farming' /> in the sidebar, then click <MockButton type='card-farming-action' />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Farm Specific Games

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Card Farming' /> and enabled <MockButton type='content' content='Farm games in queue' />
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='card-farming' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Go to the <MockButton type='all-games' /> tab and click the <MockButton type='context-add' /> button on individual games to add them to your queue
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Switch to the <MockButton type='content' content='Queue' /> tab to review what you've added, then click <MockButton type='card-farming-action' />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Card Farming Tabs

* **Games With Drops**: shows all games in your account with card drops remaining
* **Queue**: games you've specifically chosen to farm
* **Blacklisted**: games excluded from farming entirely, [read more](/docs/features/card-farming/blacklisting-games)
