# How to Manage Steam Achievements Manually

The Achievement Manager lets you manually unlock and lock achievements for any game in your library, as well as modify game statistics. Unlike the [Achievement Unlocker](/docs/features/achievement-unlocker), this is a one-by-one manual tool: useful for targeting specific achievements without automation.

### Achievements

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='your-games' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click the <MockButton type='achievement-manager' /> button on individual games to open them in the achievement manager
  </Step>

  <Step>
    On the achievement manager screen you will see a list of achievements for that game, if any are available
    
    Click <MockButton type='unlock' /> or <MockButton type='lock' /> next to an achievement to unlock or lock it individually
    
    Click  <MockButton type='unlock-all' /> or <MockButton type='lock-all' /> in the page header to affect every achievement at once

    Use the <MockButton type='checkbox' /> next to each achievement to toggle its state, then click <MockButton type='content' content='Save changes' /> to apply the changes
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type='info'>
    Some achievements/statistics are marked **Protected** and can't be modified. See [Special Flags](/docs/features/achievement-manager/special-flags)
</Callout>

### Statistics

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='your-games' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click the <MockButton type='achievement-manager' /> button on individual games to open them in the achievement manager
  </Step>

  <Step>
    On the achievement manager screen, click the <MockButton type='content' content='Statistics' /> tab in the page header. You will see a list of statistics for that game, if any are available
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Change the `value` of any statistics you want to modify and click <MockButton type='save-changes' />
  </Step>
</Steps>
