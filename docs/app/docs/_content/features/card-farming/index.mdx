# How to Farm Steam Trading Cards Automatically

The Card Farming feature allows you to automatically farm trading cards from your Steam games without needing to download or play them. This is ideal for users looking to collect cards for badge crafting or marketplace sales efficiently.

<DocsCTA />

<Callout type="info">
    You must add your [Steam Credentials](/docs/steam-credentials) before you can use this feature
</Callout>

### Farm Games In The Card Farming List Only

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Enable [`Farm Games In List`](/docs/settings/card-farming#farm-games-in-list) in <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Card Farming' />
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='card-farming' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>
  
  <Step>
    Go to the <MockButton type='all-games' /> tab and click on individual games to add and remove them
  </Step>

  <Step>  
    Click <MockButton type='content' content='Done' /> and then <MockButton type='card-farming-action' />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Farm All Games With Drops Remaining

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Enable [`Farm All Games`](/docs/settings/card-farming#farm-games-in-list) in <MockButton type='context-cog' content='Settings → Card Farming' />
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='card-farming' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Optionally, go to the <MockButton type='all-games' /> tab and click <MockButton type='content' content='Blacklist' /> on individual games to blacklist them from being farmed
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='card-farming-action' />
  </Step>
</Steps>
