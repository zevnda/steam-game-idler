# What is Steam Game Idler PRO?
First and foremost, Steam Game Idler is completely free to use, and always will be. All of the core features of SGI are available to all users at no cost.

Steam Game Idler **PRO** is an optional way to support the development of SGI. To thank you for your support, **PRO** subscribers receive a range of additional benefits that enhance the SGI experience.

There are two subscription tiers available — **Casual** and **Gamer** — each unlocking a different set of benefits.

By subscribing to **PRO**, you help with the ongoing development and maintenance of SGI, ensuring that it continues to improve and evolve over time.

| Feature | Casual ($2/mo) | Gamer ($4/mo) |
|---------|:--------------:|:--------------:|
| Ad-free experience | <Yes /> | <Yes /> |
| Exclusive themes | <Yes /> | <Yes /> |
| Unique Discord chat role | <Yes /> | <Yes /> |
| Real-time live support | <Yes /> | <Yes /> |
| Automatic Steam credentials retrieval | <No /> | <Yes /> |
| Automatic free game redemption | <No /> | <Yes /> |
| Automatic games list updates | <No /> | <Yes /> |
| Automatic card farming | <No /> | <Yes /> |
| Sell duplicate inventory items | <No /> | <Yes /> |
| Import achievement unlock timings | <No /> | <Yes /> |

<p className='text-xs text-muted-foreground -mt-4'>* Prices are in USD and exclude taxes. Billed monthly, cancel anytime.</p>

## FAQ

<Accordions type='multiple'>
  <Accordion title='How to Subscribe to PRO' id='how-to-subscribe'>
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        Open Steam Game Idler
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Click the "**Go PRO**" badge in the app's title bar
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Select your desired subscription tier *(Casual or Gamer)*
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Click the "**Get Started**" button and complete your purchase through the Stripe secure payment gateway
      </Step>
      <Step>
        You will receive an email containing your **license key**
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Open Steam Game Idler, navigate to **Settings → Subscription**, paste your license key into the input field, and click "**Activate**"

        Your license key is tied to one device at a time. To transfer it to a new device, activate it on the new device and confirm the transfer when prompted.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='How to Cancel PRO Subscription' id='how-to-cancel'>
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        Open Steam Game Idler
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Click "**Settings**" in the sidebar
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Navigate to the "**Subscription**" tab
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Click the "**Manage Subscription**" button
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Follow the prompts to confirm your cancellation
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    After cancellation, you will retain access to **PRO** benefits until the end of your current billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Transferring to a New Device' id='transfer-device'>
    Your license key is tied to one device at a time. To transfer it to a new device:
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        Open Steam Game Idler on your **new device**
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Navigate to **Settings → Subscription**
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Enter your license key and click "**Activate**"
      </Step>
      <Step>
        A prompt will appear asking you to confirm the transfer — click "**Transfer to this device**"
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    Your subscription will immediately become active on the new device. The previous device will lose access on its next launch.

    If you reinstalled the app on the **same machine**, your key will activate without any transfer prompt as the device is recognised by its hardware ID.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Switch Between PRO Tiers' id='switch-tiers'>
    To switch between **PRO** tiers, simply cancel your current subscription and subscribe to your desired tier following the standard subscription process.
  </Accordion>

    Chargebacks are when a payment made on your credit card or bank account is reversed, typically when the customer disputes a charge or claims it was unauthorized.
