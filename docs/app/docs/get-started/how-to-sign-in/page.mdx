# Sign In

<Callout type='info'>
    If you sign in to multiple accounts in the Steam client, all signed in accounts will be available on the account selection screen in SGI
</Callout>

## Steps

<Steps>
    ### Install Steam
    Download and install the [Steam client](https://store.steampowered.com/about)

    ### Sign In
    Sign in to your Steam account via the Steam client

    ### Select Your Account
    Open SGI and choose your account from the `account selection` screen to proceed to the `dashboard`
