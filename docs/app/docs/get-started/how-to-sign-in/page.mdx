# Sign In

<Callout type='warning'>
    You must have the [Steam client](https://store.steampowered.com/about) installed, running, and signed in to at least one account
</Callout>

<Callout type='info'>
    If you are signed in to more than one Steam account in the Steam client, SGI will let you choose from all of them on the account selection screen.
</Callout>

## Steps

<Steps>
    ### Install Steam
    Download and install the [Steam client](https://store.steampowered.com/about)

    ### Sign In
    Sign in to your Steam account via the Steam client

    ### Select Your Account
    Open SGI and choose your account from the account selection screen to continue
</Steps>
