### Improved 🚀
- **Games List Auto Updates**: For non-PRO users, the games list will now automatically update when SGI is launched. This should help with some of the confusion around why users aren't seeing newly purchased games being visible in the app
  - Users can still manually update their games list via the "**Refresh**" button at the top of the "**Your Games**" page
  - SGI will still continue to automatically update PRO user's games list on launch and every 15 minutes thereafter
