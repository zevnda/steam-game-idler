### New 🎉
- **Subscriptions Now Use License Keys**: Instead of being tied to a specific Steam ID, your PRO subscription is now activated with a personal license key. This means it works across the entire app, no matter which Steam account you're signed into SGI with
  - New subscribers will receive their license key via email after checkout
  - Existing subscribers are migrated automatically when launching SGI, and your key will be emailed to you for safekeeping
  - Enter your license key in **Settings → Subscription** and click **Activate**
  - License keys can be activated on one device at a time. If you change devices, activate the key on the new one and confirm the transfer when prompted
- A new **Subscription** tab has been added to **Settings**, giving you a single place to view and manage your PRO subscription
