### New 🎉
- Added per-achievement checkboxes to the "**Achievement Manager**" list, enabling bulk selection. A new "**Apply Changes**" button lets you unlock or lock all selected achievements in a single action
- Added 3 new settings to the "**Card Farming**" settings page:
  - **Unplayed Games Only**: Restrict card farming to games with zero playtime
  - **Highest Drops First**: Farm games with the most card drops remaining first
  - **Lowest Drops First**: Farm games with the fewest card drops remaining first

### Improved 🚀
- Removed the 99,999 cap on value inputs in the "**Statistics**" tab of "**Achievement Manager**"
- Refreshed the achievement list layout in the "**Custom Order**" modal for "**Achievement Unlocker**" by adding a **select/deselect all** header checkbox, and already-unlocked achievements are now visually distinct with their controls disabled
- **Achievement Unlocker** now waits 2 minutes before switching to the next game in the queue, preventing two achievements from different games being unlocked in quick succession
