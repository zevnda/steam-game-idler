# Blacklisting Games

You can blacklist specific games from being farmed for cards by going to the <MockButton type='all-games' /> tab and then clicking the <MockButton type='content' content='Blacklist' /> button next to individual games.

Blacklisted games will not be farmed for cards in either mode, even if they have card drops remaining, and even if you have added them to your card farming list.
