# How to Boost Steam Game Playtime

The Playtime Booster lets you idle one or more Steam games simultaneously to increase their playtime hours. This is useful for reaching the playtime needed to start receiving trading card drops, padding your Steam profile stats, or simply keeping a game "running" in the background.

<DocsCTA />

<Callout type="info">
  You can idle a maximum of 32 games simultaneously. This limit is set by the Steam client.
</Callout>

## Starting Idle

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='your-games' /> in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='start-idle' /> below a game's image to begin idling it. You can do this for multiple games
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click <MockButton type='idling-games' /> in the sidebar to see all games currently being idled, along with how long each has been running
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Stopping Idle

To stop idling a specific game, click the stop button on its card in either <MockButton type='your-games' /> or <MockButton type='idling-games' />.

To stop all idling games at once, go to <MockButton type='idling-games' /> and click <MockButton type='content' content='Stop All' />.

## Additional Information

When you idle a game, SGI opens a background `SteamUtility.exe (idle)` process for each game to register as active with Steam. **Do not close these windows manually** — they will close on their own when you stop idling or exit SGI.

Playtime Booster only increases hours — it does not automatically unlock achievements or farm trading cards. For those, see [Achievement Unlocker](/docs/features/achievement-unlocker) and [Card Farming](/docs/features/card-farming).
